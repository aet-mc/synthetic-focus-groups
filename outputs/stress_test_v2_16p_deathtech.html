Executive Summary

NO-GO

The AI-powered funeral planning service concept generated mixed reactions, with low purchase intent driven by concerns around trust, risk, and value. While participants found the concept unique, they expressed reservations about its believability and relevance, particularly regarding the digital twin aspect. A key barrier to adoption appears to be the perceived high cost relative to the value offered, especially when compared to traditional funeral services. Focus should be placed on building trust and demonstrating the value proposition to overcome skepticism and justify the price point.

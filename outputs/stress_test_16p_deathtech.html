Executive Summary

ITERATE

The AI-powered funeral planning service concept generated mixed reactions, primarily driven by concerns around trust, risk, and value. While some participants appreciated the uniqueness and potential for positive remembrance, a significant portion expressed hesitation about the believability and appropriateness of interacting with a digital representation of the deceased. The high price point, particularly for the premium package with the digital twin, further fueled concerns about value perception. To improve concept viability, focus should be placed on building trust, clearly demonstrating value, and addressing ethical considerations related to AI-driven memorialization.

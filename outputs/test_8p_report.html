Executive Summary

ITERATE

The coffee subscription concept resonated with participants as relevant, but purchase intent and overall appeal scores were low. Participants expressed a strong preference for established coffee habits and brands, indicating a reluctance to switch. Perceived risk associated with the subscription, particularly concerns about taste preferences and potential waste, further dampened enthusiasm. To improve appeal, offering greater control over roast and origin selection, along with a satisfaction guarantee, may mitigate risk and increase purchase likelihood.

  • This study used AI-generated personas grounded in US Census demographic data.
